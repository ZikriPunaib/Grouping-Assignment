Background

Braille’s Kitchen is a business that based by Braille’s Kitchen hq. It is a solely business that sells packed foods.

The company is active with its promotion on social media such as, Instagram and Facebook. It serves instant food which is a package that contains rice and also its main dish.

In the beginning of the business, there are two main dish that Braille’s Kitchen serves which are Ayam Merah Rumah Keduri and Daging Hitam Rumah Kenduri.

Braille’s Kitchen started to operate on 2018. It starts with a few staffs only as its not easy to find staff that have full commitments to their work.

Slowly, Braille’s Kitchen started to expand it business to every state of Malaysia successfully. Now, the Braille’s Kitchen hq are trying to expand their wings to other countries.
